About

I sit at the seam between accounting rigor and quantitative tooling — and I build on both sides.

I'm a Business Administration — Information Systems graduate from UC Riverside (3.96 GPA), currently an experienced M&A audit staff at Hood & Strong LLP, where I run substantive and controls testing across cash, fixed assets, A/P, expenses, and payroll for clients with ~$300M+ in revenue.

Before public accounting I spent three summers in real estate investment — building DCF and IRR models, underwriting $25M+ in deals, and running comparative market analyses at Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices and a family office. Alongside that I led a student investment fund as Chief Investment Officer and consulted for early-stage clients through Highlander Consulting Group.

Lately I've been channelling that into code: an end-to-end automated equity-trading system on the Alpaca API, an NLP screener over SEC EDGAR filings, and quantitative finance models in R. This site collects that work — and a space for the creative content I'm building next.
